What About Me & What I Do

I'm a 52 year old freelance designer from United States. I specialize in Website Design and Interface design (iPhone/iPad ready!) with 6 years of experience under my belt. I design with simplicity, usability and beauty in mind. If it isn't easy to use and very beautiful, it probably won't work. If you are a fellow Web or App dev I strongly believe we will work well together...

I will design the simple, usable and beautiful interface that your project needs. I’m a perfectionist, so no corner cutting, no pixel wasting and no details unpolished. I really want every site I design to turn heads and get people talking about your project. I will help you achieve your goals, and stand out against those pesky competitive markets, with stunning and effective design.

I’ve worked with a lot of people in my time as a freelancer, and that includes a lot of fellow freelancers and developers. I like to work with people who are passionate about their work. If this is yourself, get in touch ASAP for a chat about your project! I work full time freelance, happily from my home in Northern England, so expect to receive a reply rather quickly.
